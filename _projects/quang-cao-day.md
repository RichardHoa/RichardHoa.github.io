This approach allows non-technical team members to update the database directly without needing to navigate the complexities of a standard database system.

## Capability

If you are Vietnamese, this website offers a unique opportunity to explore and experience its features tailored specifically for the community. The platform provides an in-depth introduction to its purpose, capabilities, and how it integrates cultural and historical elements. You'll find a detailed guide to navigate its functionalities and make the most out of your experience.

For those who are not Vietnamese, the AI powering this website is inspired by the historic Nữ Giới Chung newspaper, first published in Vietnam around 1918. This pioneering publication was one of the first to focus on women’s issues, advocating for education, equality, and social reform. The AI adopts the distinctive voice and writing style of this cultural artifact, reviving its ethos in a modern digital format.

One feature of the website is a creative page where users can input a brand name. The AI will then generate a concise advertisement, under 150 words, styled in the voice of the Nữ Giới Chung. These ads reflect the publication’s historical tone—graceful, persuasive, and imbued with cultural nuances.
