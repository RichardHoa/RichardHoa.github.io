Quang Cao Day project

Introduction

The AI powering this website is inspired by the historic Nữ Giới Chung newspaper, first published in Vietnam around 1918. This pioneering publication was one of the first to focus on women’s issues, advocating for education, equality, and social reform. The AI adopts the distinctive voice and writing style of this cultural artifact, create a distinct, 150 words short ads about any brand name

Instead of a traditional database, the site leverages Google Sheets, which suits its temporary nature and simplifies data management. This approach allows non-technical team members to update the database directly without needing to navigate the complexities of a standard database system.

you can visit the site through the link quangcaoday.nghiencuuvietnam.com.

Technology

  • Frontend: React with MUI Library
  • Backend: Serverless function in Vercel
  • Database: GG sheets
